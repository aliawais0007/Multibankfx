About MultiBank Group
MultiBank was established in California, USA in 2005. We maintain offices worldwide including but not limited to Sydney, Los Angeles, Frankfurt, Vienna, Barcelona, Dubai, Beijing, Guangzhou, Limassol (Cyprus) and others. Our Group is heavily regulated worldwide by:
  • The Australian Securities and Investment Commission ("ASIC")
  • The Federal Financial Supervisory Authority ("BaFin") of Germany
  • The Financial Market Authority ("FMA") of Austria
  • The National Securities Market Commission ("CNMV") of Spain
  • The Financial Services Comission ("FSC") of the BVI
  • The Cayman Islands Monetary Authority ("CIMA")
